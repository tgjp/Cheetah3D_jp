Drag Force

Drag Force effect or objects emulate the real world behavior of an object (or particle in the case of this effect or) passing through air or liquid. Multiple effect or types can be used to create even more complex behavior


Drag Force effect or object used with a particle emitter to slow the flow of particles..

Properties

  • Strength: Strength of the drag force.
  • Size: The size of the range.
  • Geometry: The shape of the range.
  • Invert: Inverts the range.
