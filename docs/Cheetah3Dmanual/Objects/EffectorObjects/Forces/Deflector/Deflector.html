Deflector Force

Deflector Force effector objects bounce (deflect) particles, thus emulating rigid body dynamics.


Deflector Force effector object used in conjunction with a Gravity Force effector object to repel particles.

Properties

  • Damping: Damping of the particle speed after every bounce.
  • Strength: Strength of the force.
  • Attenuation: Attenuation of the force.
  • Size: The size of the range.
  • Geometry: The shape of the range.
  • Invert: Inverts the range.
