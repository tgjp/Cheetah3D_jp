Attraction Force

The Attraction Force effector object emulates the real work force of attraction. Placing a Attraction Force effector object in a scene with particles will apply the force to all particles.


Attraction Force effector object use to draw particles emitted from a Particle Emitter object.

Properties

  • Strength: Strength of the force.
  • Attenuation: Attenuation of the force.
  • Size: The size of the range.
  • Geometry: The shape of the range.
  • Invert: Inverts the range.
